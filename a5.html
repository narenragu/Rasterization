A5: 3D Rendering using Matrix transformations

Instructions / Notes

  • Change camera position using the 3 sliders under Camera Settings
  • Modify settings related to rendering under Render Settings
  • To switch objects, use the dropdown to select between "Suzanne", "Teapot", and "Cube"
  • Note: The directional light is visualized as the floating yellow wireframe sphere, and can be hidden by unchecking Show Directional Light? under Light Settings
  • Note: Light Follows Camera? will make the directional light point in the same direction the camera is pointing

  • Note (for grading): The Camera and Directional Light are following a circular parametric path.
